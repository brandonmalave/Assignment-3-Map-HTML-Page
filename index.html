What Trumps proposed China Tariffs could cost US States

Data from the U.S. Census Bureau shows which states would be most affected by a potential Trump tariff.

by Brandon Malave

December 20, 2024

California, Illinois, and Texas could be spending billions of dollars in tariffs if Trump goes forward with his proposed 10% tariff on imported goods from China.

According to a post President Elect Donald Trump made on Truth Social, Trump claims that there are is massive amount of drugs, such as fentanyl, being imported into the United States from China. Trump wrote that the 10% additional tariff on imported goods from China will remain until drugs stop coming into the country from China.

The chloropeth map below shows the total value of commodities imported into the United States from China by state in 2024 through October. California would be the most affected by Trumps proposed tariff, and would have cost all importers more than $10 billion dollars.
